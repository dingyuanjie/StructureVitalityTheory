## 摘要 / Abstract

**中文（Chinese）：**

结构生力理论是一种旨在打通自然科学与社会科学、技术系统与人类行为之间关系的跨学科方法论。它提出：结构本身蕴含推动系统演化的“生力”，而这种生力决定了系统的稳定性、变迁路径与发展极限。无论是物理中的气体扩散、社会中的意识形态传播，还是经济系统的资本分布，都体现了结构如何决定行为、能量如何在结构中流动。本文通过结构场、动力场与边界条件三元模型，构建了一个可用于全领域观察、解释和预测的认知框架，并借助统计力学类比、东方哲学融汇，揭示结构生力背后深层次的统一逻辑。结构生力不仅是一种理论，更是一种方法论与文明路径选择，为人类社会的协调、演化与大同提供思想支柱。

**英文（English）：**

The Theory of Structural Vitality is an interdisciplinary framework that bridges natural sciences with social sciences, and technical systems with human dynamics. It posits that structure itself contains an inherent "vital force" (shengli) which drives systemic evolution, defines limits of stability, and shapes the trajectory of change. From gas diffusion in physics, to ideological propagation in societies, to capital distribution in economic systems — all exhibit how structure conditions behavior and energy flows within it. By introducing a triadic model of Structural Fields, Dynamic Forces, and Boundary Conditions, this paper offers a unifying lens for observation, explanation, and foresight across all domains. Rooted in analogies to statistical mechanics and informed by Eastern philosophical wisdom, the theory reveals a deeper logic behind systemic transformation. Structural Vitality is not just a theory—it is a methodology and civilizational proposition for global harmony, sustainable evolution, and eventual convergence toward universal concord.
