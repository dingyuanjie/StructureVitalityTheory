## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
音乐不仅是声音的排列，也是时间与结构的艺术。本文基于“结构生力”理论，探讨黄金比例、斐波那契数列在音乐旋律、节奏和和声中的应用。通过分析结构生力对音乐稳定性、节奏感及情感共鸣的作用，揭示音乐中的自然螺旋规律和生力分布如何影响听者的心理感受，为作曲、音乐设计及声音艺术提供理论指导。  

**English**:  
Music is not only an arrangement of sounds but also an art of time and structure. Based on Structural Vital Force theory, this paper explores the application of the golden ratio and Fibonacci sequence in musical melody, rhythm, and harmony. By analyzing the role of Structural Vital Force in musical stability, rhythm, and emotional resonance, it reveals how natural helical patterns and force distributions in music influence listener perception, providing theoretical guidance for composition, music design, and sound art.
