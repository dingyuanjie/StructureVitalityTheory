# 结构生力视角下的宇宙尺度结构演化趋势  
## Structural Evolution Trends at Cosmic Scales from the Structural Vital Force Perspective

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
宇宙结构的演化从微观粒子到宏观星系、星系团及大尺度宇宙网呈现螺旋耦合与动力学自组织特征。本文基于“结构生力”理论，探讨宇宙尺度的结构演化趋势，包括能量分布、物质聚集、螺旋模式形成及涌现现象。通过构建多尺度螺旋耦合模型，分析引力、暗物质与暗能量作用下的演化规律，提出宇宙结构长期演化的趋势预测，为理解大爆炸后的宇宙结构形成和未来演化提供理论框架。  

**English**:  
The evolution of cosmic structures, from microscopic particles to galaxies, galaxy clusters, and the large-scale cosmic web, exhibits helical coupling and dynamical self-organization. Based on Structural Vital Force theory, this paper explores structural evolution trends at cosmic scales, including energy distribution, matter aggregation, helical pattern formation, and emergent phenomena. By constructing multi-scale helical coupling models, we analyze evolutionary patterns under the influence of gravity, dark matter, and dark energy, proposing trend predictions for long-term cosmic structure evolution and providing a theoretical framework for understanding post-Big Bang structure formation and future development.

## 一、多尺度螺旋耦合模型 (Multi-Scale Helical Coupling Model)

**中文**：  
- 宇宙结构可分为微观、星系及宏观大尺度层级，每一层级均受结构生力驱动；  
- 螺旋耦合实现能量、物质与信息在不同尺度的自组织传递；  
- 局部结构生力状态决定物质聚集速率及星系形成模式。  

**English**:  
- Cosmic structures are divided into microscopic, galactic, and large-scale layers, each driven by Structural Vital Force.  
- Helical coupling enables self-organized transfer of energy, matter, and information across scales.  
- Local force states determine matter aggregation rates and galaxy formation patterns.
