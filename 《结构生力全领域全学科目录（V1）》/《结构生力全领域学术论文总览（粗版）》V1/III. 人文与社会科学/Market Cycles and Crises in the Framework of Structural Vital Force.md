# III. 人文与社会科学  
## 10. 经济学

# Market Cycles and Crises in the Framework of Structural Vital Force

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
将资本流动、市场波动建模为结构生力驱动的非线性系统，预测泡沫形成与破裂的临界点。  

**English**:  
Capital flows and market fluctuations are modeled as nonlinear systems driven by Structural Vital Force, allowing prediction of critical points for bubble formation and collapse.

---

## 验证思路 (Validation Approach)

**中文**：  
全球金融数据回测：收集历史股市、债市、商品市场数据，通过结构生力模型计算市场能量流、关键节点压力及临界阈值，验证模型对泡沫周期及金融危机的预测精度。  

**English**:  
Backtesting global financial data: compile historical data from equity, bond, and commodity markets, use Structural Vital Force models to calculate market energy flows, key node pressures, and critical thresholds, and validate the model's predictive accuracy for bubble cycles and financial crises.

---

## 理论公式示例 (Example Theoretical Formulas)

**中文**：  
市场能量—波动方程：  
\[
\frac{dM_i}{dt} = \sum_j B_{ij} \cdot g(M_j, \phi_{ij}) - \delta_i M_i
\]  
其中 \(M_i\) 为市场节点能量，\(B_{ij}\) 为市场耦合强度，\(\phi_{ij}\) 为结构生力角度因子，\(\delta_i\) 为市场耗散系数。  

临界泡沫阈值：  
\[
M_i^{crit} = \lambda \cdot \sum_j g(\phi_{ij}) \cdot M_j
\]  
当 \(M_i > M_i^{crit}\) 时，市场可能触发泡沫破裂或危机扩散。  

**English**:  
Market energy–fluctuation equation:  
\[
\frac{dM_i}{dt} = \sum_j B_{ij} \cdot g(M_j, \phi_{ij}) - \delta_i M_i
\]  
where \(M_i\) is market node energy, \(B_{ij}\) is market coupling strength, \(\phi_{ij}\) is Structural Vital Force angle factor, and \(\delta_i\) is market dissipation coefficient.  

Critical bubble threshold:  
\[
M_i^{crit} = \lambda \cdot \sum_j g(\phi_{ij}) \cdot M_j
\]  
When \(M_i > M_i^{crit}\), the market may trigger bubble collapse or crisis propagation.

## 研究展望 (Research Outlook)

**中文**：  
- 将结构生力模型应用于实时金融市场监测和风险预警；  
- 探索政策干预对关键节点能量分布与市场韧性的影响；  
- 推动跨学科金融系统研究，将复杂系统科学与经济学整合。  

**English**:  
- Apply Structural Vital Force models for real-time financial market monitoring and risk forecasting.  
- Explore the impact of policy interventions on key node energy distribution and market resilience.  
- Promote interdisciplinary research integrating complex systems science and economics.
