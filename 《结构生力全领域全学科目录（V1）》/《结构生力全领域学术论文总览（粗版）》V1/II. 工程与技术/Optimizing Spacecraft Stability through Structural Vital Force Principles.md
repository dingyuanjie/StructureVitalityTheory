# II. 工程与技术  
## 6. 航空航天工程

# Optimizing Spacecraft Stability through Structural Vital Force Principles

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
基于结构生力原理设计航天器螺旋支撑架与轨道修正策略，通过螺旋结构优化能量传递路径，提升航天器抗微陨石冲击能力和姿态保持精度，为长期轨道任务提供稳定性保障。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft spiral support structures and orbital correction strategies are designed based on Structural Vital Force principles. By optimizing energy transfer paths through spiral configurations, spacecraft impact resistance against micrometeoroids and attitude control precision are enhanced, ensuring stability for long-duration orbital missions.

---

## 验证思路 (Validation Approach)

**中文**：  
1. 风洞实验：测试航天器在气动力扰动下的姿态稳定性；  
2. 微陨石冲击模拟：利用动力学仿真分析螺旋支撑架对冲击能量的吸收与分散；  
3. 轨道力学仿真：模拟姿态控制策略在多变轨道环境下的稳态响应。  

**English**:  
1. Wind tunnel testing: assess spacecraft attitude stability under aerodynamic disturbances.  
2. Micrometeoroid impact simulation: use dynamic modeling to analyze energy absorption and dissipation by spiral support structures.  
3. Orbital mechanics simulation: simulate steady-state responses of attitude control strategies in varying orbital environments.

## 理论公式示例 (Example Theoretical Formulas)

**中文**：  
螺旋支撑架振动能量吸收公式：  
\[
E_{abs} = \sum_i k_i \cdot (\theta_i - \theta_0)^2
\]  
其中 \(k_i\) 为局部结构刚度，\(\theta_i\) 为螺旋角偏移，\(\theta_0\) 为初始螺旋角。  

姿态控制修正方程：  
\[
\frac{d\vec{\omega}}{dt} = I^{-1} \left(\vec{T}_{control} + \alpha \cdot \vec{F}_{SVF} \right)
\]  
其中 \(\vec{\omega}\) 为角速度矢量，\(I\) 为惯性矩阵，\(\vec{F}_{SVF}\) 为结构生力修正项。  

**English**:  
Vibrational energy absorption in spiral support structures:  
\[
E_{abs} = \sum_i k_i \cdot (\theta_i - \theta_0)^2
\]  
where \(k_i\) is local structural stiffness, \(\theta_i\) is spiral angle deviation, and \(\theta_0\) is initial spiral angle.  

Attitude control correction equation:  
\[
\frac{d\vec{\omega}}{dt} = I^{-1} \left(\vec{T}_{control} + \alpha \cdot \vec{F}_{SVF} \right)
\]  
where \(\vec{\omega}\) is angular velocity vector, \(I\) is inertia matrix, and \(\vec{F}_{SVF}\) is the Structural Vital Force correction term.

## 研究展望 (Research Outlook)

**中文**：  
- 将结构生力优化方法扩展至大型深空探测器，提高长期任务的抗扰动能力；  
- 结合人工智能与仿真优化螺旋结构布局，实现自适应姿态控制；  
- 为未来星际航行器提供理论与工程支撑，探索多尺度结构生力应用。  

**English**:  
- Extend Structural Vital Force optimization methods to large deep-space probes to enhance long-duration disturbance resistance.  
- Integrate AI and simulation to optimize spiral structural layouts for adaptive attitude control.  
- Provide theoretical and engineering support for future interstellar spacecraft, exploring multi-scale applications of Structural Vital Force.
