# IV. 跨学科与未来学  
## 12. 复杂系统科学

# Universality of Structural Vital Force in Complex Networks

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
提出结构生力在自然、技术、社会系统中的普适性规律，预测网络崩溃与自组织的统一临界条件。  

**English**:  
The universality of Structural Vital Force is proposed across natural, technological, and social systems, predicting unified critical conditions for network collapse and self-organization.

---

## 验证思路 (Validation Approach)

**中文**：  
跨领域数据集的临界性分析：收集生物网络、交通网络、金融网络等数据，通过结构生力网络模型计算关键节点权重、螺旋结构角及能量分布，分析网络临界点与自组织模式。  

**English**:  
Criticality analysis across cross-domain datasets: collect data from biological, transportation, and financial networks, use Structural Vital Force network model to calculate key node weights, spiral structure angles, and energy distribution, and analyze network critical points and self-organization patterns.

---

## 理论公式示例 (Example Theoretical Formulas)

**中文**：  
网络节点能量演化方程：  
\[
\frac{dE_i}{dt} = \sum_j C_{ij} \cdot f(E_j, \theta_{ij}) - \epsilon_i E_i
\]  
其中 \(E_i\) 为节点能量，\(C_{ij}\) 为耦合强度，\(\theta_{ij}\) 为结构生力角度，\(\epsilon_i\) 为能量耗散系数。  

临界条件判定：  
\[
\sum_i E_i \ge E_{crit} \implies \text{网络崩溃}
\]  
\(\theta_{ij}\) 分布优化可促进自组织稳定态的形成。  

**English**:  
Node energy evolution equation:  
\[
\frac{dE_i}{dt} = \sum_j C_{ij} \cdot f(E_j, \theta_{ij}) - \epsilon_i E_i
\]  
where \(E_i\) is node energy, \(C_{ij}\) is coupling strength, \(\theta_{ij}\) is Structural Vital Force angle, and \(\epsilon_i\) is energy dissipation coefficient.  

Critical condition:  
\[
\sum_i E_i \ge E_{crit} \implies \text{network collapse}
\]  
Optimizing \(\theta_{ij}\) distribution promotes formation of stable self-organized states.
