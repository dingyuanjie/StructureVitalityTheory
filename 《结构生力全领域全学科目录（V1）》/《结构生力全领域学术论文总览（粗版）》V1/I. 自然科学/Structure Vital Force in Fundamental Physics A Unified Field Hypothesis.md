## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
提出结构生力作为物理学基本作用力的潜在第五种形式。通过引入螺旋结构角与能量密度的函数关系，修正经典引力方程及量子场波动方程。该理论预测在特定能量尺度下，粒子质量可能出现微小漂移，能级结构存在可测的异常，为高能物理实验提供新的解释框架。  

**English**:  
We propose Structural Vital Force as a potential fifth fundamental interaction in physics. By introducing a functional relationship between spiral structural angles and energy density, classical gravitational equations and quantum field wave equations are modified. The theory predicts subtle particle mass shifts and measurable energy level anomalies at specific energy scales, offering a new explanatory framework for high-energy physics experiments.

## 验证思路 (Validation Approach)

**中文**：  
1. 高能对撞实验数据对比：利用大型强子对撞机（LHC）及未来对撞设施的粒子质量谱与能级分布数据。  
2. 理论方程拟合：将结构生力修正后的引力与量子场方程预测结果，与实验观测进行统计拟合。  
3. 异常标记与偏差分析：分析质量漂移和能级异常的概率分布，寻找与标准模型偏离的证据。  

**English**:  
1. Comparison with high-energy collision data: Utilize particle mass spectra and energy level distributions from the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) and future collider facilities.  
2. Theoretical equation fitting: Compare predictions from Structural Vital Force-modified gravitational and quantum field equations with experimental observations using statistical fitting.  
3. Anomaly detection and deviation analysis: Examine the probability distribution of mass shifts and energy level anomalies to identify evidence of deviations from the Standard Model.
