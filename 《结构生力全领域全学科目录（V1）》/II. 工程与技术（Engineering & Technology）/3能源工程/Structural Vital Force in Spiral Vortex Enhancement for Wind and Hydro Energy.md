# 结构生力在风能/水能螺旋涡流增强中的应用  
## Structural Vital Force in Spiral Vortex Enhancement for Wind and Hydro Energy

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
风能与水能利用效率受限于流体动力学特性与涡流结构。传统涡轮设计依赖经验和数值模拟，难以充分捕获螺旋流场潜能。本文提出“结构生力”作为跨尺度几何驱动力，通过螺旋和层级结构优化风轮叶片、涡轮通道及水流引导结构，实现涡旋稳定、能量集中和流场强化。生力网络提供流场优化指标，为风能与水能的高效捕获提供理论支持。  

**English**:  
The efficiency of wind and hydro energy is limited by fluid dynamic characteristics and vortex structures. Traditional turbine designs rely on empirical rules and numerical simulations, struggling to fully exploit the potential of spiral flow fields. This paper introduces Structural Vital Force as a cross-scale geometric driving principle, optimizing turbine blades, vortex channels, and flow guiding structures through helical and hierarchical designs to achieve vortex stabilization, energy concentration, and flow enhancement. The force network provides flow optimization metrics, offering theoretical support for high-efficiency wind and hydro energy capture.

---

## 一、螺旋涡流结构优化 (Spiral Vortex Structure Optimization)

**中文**：  
螺旋涡流决定风轮与水轮能量捕获效率：  
- 生力网络量化涡核强度和流体应力分布；  
- 螺旋叶片和涡道结构提高能量转化效率；  
- 层级流场优化局部涡旋稳定性，减少能量损耗。  

**English**:  
Spiral vortices determine the energy capture efficiency of wind and water turbines:  
- The force network quantifies vortex core strength and fluid stress distribution.  
- Helical blade and vortex channel structures improve energy conversion efficiency.  
- Hierarchical flow field optimization stabilizes local vortices, reducing energy loss.

---

## 二、流场强化与能量集中 (Flow Field Enhancement and Energy Concentration)

**中文**：  
涡旋流场可实现能量集中与传输优化：  
- 生力网络指导流体路径设计，使能量在涡核集中；  
- 螺旋通道和分级结构支持涡旋间能量耦合，提高整体效率；  
- 动态调整涡流参数，实现风速或水流波动下的稳定能量捕获。  

**English**:  
Vortex flow fields enable energy concentration and transport optimization:  
- The force network guides fluid path design, concentrating energy in vortex cores.  
- Helical channels and hierarchical structures support energy coupling between vortices, improving overall efficiency.  
- Dynamically adjusts vortex parameters to achieve stable energy capture under variable wind or water flow.

---

## 三、涡流稳定性与设备可靠性 (Vortex Stability and Equipment Reliability)

**中文**：  
流体螺旋结构对设备长期运行稳定性影响显著：  
- 生力网络预测叶片和涡道结构在流体动力下的应力分布；  
- 螺旋与层级结构增强抗振动和抗疲劳能力；  
- 提供维护和寿命预测指标，提高风能与水能设备可靠性。  

**English**:  
Spiral fluid structures significantly affect long-term equipment stability:  
- The force network predicts stress distribution in blades and vortex channels under fluid dynamics.  
- Helical and hierarchical structures enhance vibration and fatigue resistance.  
- Provides maintenance and lifetime prediction metrics, improving wind and hydro energy equipment reliability.

## 四、展望与应用 (Outlook and Applications)

**中文**：  
未来研究方向包括：  
1. 多尺度仿真结合生力网络，实现涡旋优化和叶片设计全局提升；  
2. 开发风能与水能自适应调控系统，提高能量捕获效率与稳定性；  
3. 应用于海上风电、河流水轮和涡轮潮汐能系统，实现低损耗、高效率和长期稳定的能源收集。  

**English**:  
Future research directions include:  
1. Multi-scale simulations combined with force networks to globally optimize vortex structures and blade designs.  
2. Developing adaptive wind and hydro energy control systems to improve energy capture efficiency and stability.  
3. Applications in offshore wind, river turbines, and tidal energy systems to achieve low-loss, high-efficiency, and long-term stable energy harvesting.
