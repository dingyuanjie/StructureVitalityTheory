# 结构生力在航天器稳定姿态控制中的应用  
## Structural Vital Force in Spacecraft Attitude Stability Control

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
航天器在轨运行面临姿态稳定、惯性管理与动力消耗的多重挑战。传统姿态控制依赖反作用轮、推进器和控制算法，但难以充分利用航天器结构本身的力学优势。本文提出“结构生力”作为跨尺度几何驱动力，通过螺旋惯性分布、层级质量布局和结构耦合优化航天器姿态控制系统，实现稳定、低能耗和快速响应的姿态调节。生力网络提供姿态优化指标，为下一代高性能航天器设计提供理论指导。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft in-orbit operation faces multiple challenges including attitude stability, inertia management, and power consumption. Traditional attitude control relies on reaction wheels, thrusters, and control algorithms but struggles to fully exploit the mechanical advantages of spacecraft structures. This paper introduces Structural Vital Force as a cross-scale geometric driving principle, optimizing spacecraft attitude control systems through helical inertia distribution, hierarchical mass layouts, and structural coupling to achieve stable, low-energy, and fast-response attitude adjustment. The force network provides attitude optimization metrics, offering theoretical guidance for next-generation high-performance spacecraft design.

---

## 一、螺旋惯性与质量布局优化 (Helical Inertia and Mass Layout Optimization)

**中文**：  
航天器姿态稳定性与惯性分布密切相关：  
- 生力网络量化螺旋惯性矩和质量分布对姿态响应的影响；  
- 层级质量布局降低姿态振荡并增强恢复力；  
- 螺旋结构耦合优化旋转惯量，实现多轴稳定和快速响应。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft attitude stability is closely related to inertia distribution:  
- The force network quantifies the influence of helical inertia and mass distribution on attitude response.  
- Hierarchical mass layouts reduce attitude oscillations and enhance restoring forces.  
- Helical structural coupling optimizes rotational inertia, achieving multi-axis stability and rapid response.

---

## 二、姿态调节与控制策略优化 (Attitude Adjustment and Control Strategy Optimization)

**中文**：  
姿态调节需兼顾稳定性与能量效率：  
- 生力网络指导反作用轮与磁矩器的最优耦合策略；  
- 层级结构支持多尺度姿态控制，实现微调与快速大角度转动兼顾；  
- 螺旋分布优化动力消耗，提升长期轨道操作效率。  

**English**:  
Attitude adjustment requires balancing stability and energy efficiency:  
- The force network guides optimal coupling strategies of reaction wheels and magnetorquers.  
- Hierarchical structures support multi-scale attitude control, accommodating both fine adjustments and rapid large-angle rotations.  
- Helical distribution optimizes power consumption, improving long-term orbital operation efficiency.

---

## 三、结构耦合与抗扰动能力 (Structural Coupling and Disturbance Rejection)

**中文**：  
航天器在轨受外部扰动（如气动力、太阳辐射压、磁力）影响：  
- 生力网络预测结构耦合对扰动响应的作用；  
- 螺旋与层级布局增强抗扰动能力，减少姿态偏差；  
- 提供扰动响应优化指标，实现稳定运行与可靠控制。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft in orbit is subject to external disturbances such as aerodynamic drag, solar radiation pressure, and magnetic forces:  
- The force network predicts the effect of structural coupling on disturbance response.  
- Helical and hierarchical layouts enhance disturbance rejection, reducing attitude deviation.  
- Provides disturbance response optimization metrics, ensuring stable operation and reliable control.

---

## 四、展望与应用 (Outlook and Applications)

**中文**：  
未来研究方向包括：  
1. 多尺度仿真结合生力网络，实现从微结构惯性到整星姿态控制的全局优化；  
2. 开发自适应姿态控制系统，提高能效和响应速度；  
3. 应用于卫星、深空探测器和空间站模块，实现长期稳定、低能耗、高精度的航天器姿态控制。  

**English**:  
Future research directions include:  
1. Multi-scale simulations combined with force networks to globally optimize attitude control from micro-structural inertia to whole spacecraft dynamics.  
2. Developing adaptive attitude control systems to improve energy efficiency and response speed.  
3. Applications in satellites, deep-space probes, and space station modules to achieve long-term stable, low-energy, and high-precision spacecraft attitude control.
