# 结构生力在螺旋轨道推进中的应用  
## Structural Vital Force in Spiral-Orbit Propulsion

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
航天器轨道推进效率受限于燃料消耗、推进力方向控制和轨道几何设计。传统轨道转移依赖霍曼转移或有限数值优化，难以充分利用航天器结构本身的螺旋动力潜能。本文提出“结构生力”作为跨尺度几何驱动力，通过螺旋推进路径设计、结构耦合优化和惯性分布调整，实现推进效率提升、燃料消耗降低及轨道稳定性增强。生力网络提供轨道优化指标，为未来高效、低能耗航天器推进系统提供理论指导。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft orbital propulsion efficiency is constrained by fuel consumption, thrust vector control, and orbital geometry design. Traditional orbital transfers rely on Hohmann maneuvers or limited numerical optimization, struggling to fully exploit the helical dynamic potential inherent in spacecraft structures. This paper introduces Structural Vital Force as a cross-scale geometric driving principle, optimizing helical propulsion paths, structural coupling, and inertia distribution to enhance propulsion efficiency, reduce fuel consumption, and improve orbital stability. The force network provides orbital optimization metrics, offering theoretical guidance for next-generation high-efficiency, low-energy spacecraft propulsion systems.

---

## 一、螺旋推进路径设计 (Helical Propulsion Path Design)

**中文**：  
螺旋轨道推进依赖路径几何优化：  
- 生力网络量化螺旋路径对推进力和轨道变化的贡献；  
- 多层螺旋设计提升推进效率，减少轨道偏差；  
- 分级螺旋路径实现微调和大幅轨道转移兼顾，提高燃料利用率。  

**English**:  
Helical orbital propulsion relies on geometric path optimization:  
- The force network quantifies the contribution of helical paths to thrust and orbital changes.  
- Multi-level helical designs improve propulsion efficiency and reduce orbital deviation.  
- Hierarchical helical paths balance fine adjustments with large orbital transfers, enhancing fuel utilization.

---

## 二、结构耦合与惯性分布优化 (Structural Coupling and Inertia Distribution Optimization)

**中文**：  
航天器推进性能受惯性分布和结构耦合影响：  
- 生力网络优化惯性分布，提高推进力传递效率；  
- 螺旋结构耦合减少推进过程中的振动和能量损耗；  
- 层级质量布局支持多轴螺旋推进，实现轨道稳定性与动力效率双提升。  

**English**:  
Spacecraft propulsion performance is influenced by inertia distribution and structural coupling:  
- The force network optimizes inertia distribution to improve thrust transfer efficiency.  
- Helical structural coupling reduces vibration and energy loss during propulsion.  
- Hierarchical mass layouts support multi-axis helical propulsion, enhancing orbital stability and energy efficiency.

---

## 三、燃料消耗与推进效率优化 (Fuel Consumption and Propulsion Efficiency Optimization)

**中文**：  
螺旋推进可实现燃料节约与能量最大化利用：  
- 生力网络模拟螺旋推进下的燃料消耗分布；  
- 螺旋路径设计减少重复推进和非必要轨道偏移；  
- 提供推进效率评估指标，为低能耗轨道机动提供理论依据。  

**English**:  
Helical propulsion can achieve fuel savings and maximize energy utilization:  
- The force network simulates fuel consumption distribution under helical propulsion.  
- Helical path design reduces redundant thrusting and unnecessary orbital deviation.  
- Provides propulsion efficiency assessment metrics, offering theoretical basis for low-energy orbital maneuvers.

## 四、展望与应用 (Outlook and Applications)

**中文**：  
未来研究方向包括：  
1. 多尺度螺旋推进仿真结合生力网络，实现轨道转移和姿态控制整体优化；  
2. 开发自适应螺旋推进系统，提高燃料利用率和轨道机动灵活性；  
3. 应用于深空探测器、卫星轨道维护和空间货运系统，实现高效率、低能耗、可持续的空间推进。  

**English**:  
Future research directions include:  
1. Multi-scale helical propulsion simulations combined with force networks for integrated orbital transfer and attitude control optimization.  
2. Developing adaptive helical propulsion systems to improve fuel utilization and orbital maneuver flexibility.  
3. Applications in deep-space probes, satellite orbit maintenance, and space logistics systems to achieve high-efficiency, low-energy, and sustainable space propulsion.
