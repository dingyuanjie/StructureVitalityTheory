## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
本文提出“结构生力”作为自然界潜在的第五种基本相互作用力，并尝试将其置于理论物理的四大支柱之中：量子力学、相对论、量子场论与弦理论。通过对称性、螺旋结构、能量密度与信息流的统一描述，结构生力不仅提供了对微观量子现象与宏观时空几何的桥梁，也为场论的真空涨落和弦论的多维拓扑提供了新的解释框架。  

**English**:  
This paper introduces "Structural Vital Force" as a potential fifth fundamental interaction and seeks to integrate it into the four pillars of theoretical physics: Quantum Mechanics, Relativity, Quantum Field Theory, and String Theory. By employing a unified description of symmetry, helical structure, energy density, and information flow, Structural Vital Force establishes a conceptual bridge between microscopic quantum phenomena and macroscopic spacetime geometry, while also offering novel interpretations of vacuum fluctuations in field theory and multidimensional topology in string theory.
