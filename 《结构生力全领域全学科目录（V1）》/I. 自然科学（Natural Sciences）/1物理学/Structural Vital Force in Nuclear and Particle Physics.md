# 结构生力在核物理与粒子物理中的应用  
## Structural Vital Force in Nuclear and Particle Physics

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
本文探讨“结构生力”在核物理与粒子物理中的潜在作用。我们提出，现有的标准模型虽在实验上高度成功，但仍存在暗物质起源、质量层级问题和对称性破缺等未解之谜。通过引入结构生力这一几何化相互作用，可以修正标准模型中的不足，并提出“螺旋粒子构型”作为基本粒子的深层结构。该理论可能为统一已知基本力提供新的路径。  

**English**:  
This paper investigates the potential role of "Structural Vital Force" in nuclear and particle physics. While the Standard Model has achieved remarkable experimental success, it still faces unresolved mysteries such as the origin of dark matter, the hierarchy problem, and symmetry breaking. By introducing Structural Vital Force as a geometric interaction, we propose modifications to the Standard Model and suggest "spiral particle configurations" as the underlying structure of elementary particles. This framework may offer a novel pathway toward unifying the known fundamental forces.

## 一、标准模型的修正 (Modifications to the Standard Model)

**中文**：  
标准模型将自然界的基本相互作用统一在电弱与强相互作用框架下，但：  
- 它未能纳入引力，也难以解释暗物质和暗能量；  
- 粒子质量来源仅依赖希格斯机制，缺乏几何解释；  
- 中微子振荡与宇宙物质-反物质不对称性仍是悬而未决的问题。  

结构生力提供了补充：  
- 将基本粒子看作“螺旋构型”的稳定解，使质量与对称性破缺源于几何拓扑张力；  
- 提供额外的规范场修正，可能解释暗物质为结构生力的“几何激发态”；  
- 在夸克禁闭问题上，结构生力可作为强相互作用之外的“几何束缚机制”。  

**English**:  
The Standard Model unifies fundamental interactions within the electroweak and strong interaction framework, but:  
- It excludes gravity and fails to explain dark matter and dark energy.  
- Particle masses rely solely on the Higgs mechanism, lacking a geometric explanation.  
- Neutrino oscillations and the matter–antimatter asymmetry of the universe remain unresolved.  

Structural Vital Force offers supplements:  
- Elementary particles can be regarded as stable "spiral configurations," where mass and symmetry breaking originate from geometric–topological tension.  
- It provides additional gauge corrections, potentially explaining dark matter as "geometric excitations" of Structural Vital Force.  
- For the quark confinement problem, Structural Vital Force may serve as a geometric binding mechanism beyond strong interactions.

## 二、螺旋粒子构型 (Spiral Particle Configurations)

**中文**：  
传统粒子被视为点状或弦状对象。本文提出：  
- 基本粒子可以建模为“螺旋涡旋”态，由结构生力维持其稳定性；  
- 粒子的自旋、自旋-轨道耦合可解释为螺旋构型的自然几何效应；  
- 该构型能统一费米子与玻色子的本质，前者为单螺旋态，后者为双螺旋/对称态；  
- 螺旋构型也为解释量子场论中的真空涨落提供新途径，即真空为结构生力的零点螺旋网络。  

**English**:  
Traditionally, particles are modeled as point-like or string-like entities. Here, we propose:  
- Elementary particles can be represented as "spiral vortex" states stabilized by Structural Vital Force.  
- Particle spin and spin–orbit coupling emerge naturally from the geometry of spiral configurations.  
- This model unifies fermions and bosons: fermions as single spiral states, bosons as double spiral or symmetric states.  
- Spiral configurations also provide a new approach to quantum field vacuum fluctuations, interpreting the vacuum as a zero-point spiral network of Structural Vital Force.

## 三、展望与实验验证 (Outlook and Experimental Validation)

**中文**：  
结构生力在核与粒子物理中的应用，既是对标准模型的修正，也是迈向统一场论的一步。未来方向包括：  
1. 在高能对撞机实验中寻找“螺旋粒子构型”的迹象；  
2. 通过中微子实验检验结构生力的几何贡献；  
3. 在暗物质探测实验中，探索几何激发态是否可观测；  
4. 与弦理论和量子场论耦合，发展多尺度统一框架。  

**English**:  
The application of Structural Vital Force in nuclear and particle physics serves both as a modification of the Standard Model and as a step toward a unified field theory. Future directions include:  
1. Searching for signs of "spiral particle configurations" in high-energy collider experiments.  
2. Testing geometric contributions of Structural Vital Force in neutrino experiments.  
3. Investigating whether geometric excitations are detectable in dark matter experiments.  
4. Coupling the theory with string theory and quantum field theory to develop a multiscale unification framework.
