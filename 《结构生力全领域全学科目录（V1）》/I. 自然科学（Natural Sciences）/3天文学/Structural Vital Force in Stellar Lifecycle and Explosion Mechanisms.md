**中文**：

**English**:  
The stellar lifecycle encompasses formation, main-sequence evolution, late-stage expansion, and final explosion (supernova, neutron star, or black hole formation). Conventional theories rely on nuclear reactions, radiation pressure, gravitational collapse, and electron degeneracy pressure.
