## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
宇宙微波背景（CMB）为早期宇宙提供了温度与密度扰动的全景图，传统解释依赖暴胀理论、重子声波振荡及暗物质分布。然而，微小各向异性和大尺度结构分布的规律性仍有未解之谜。本文提出“结构生力”作为跨尺度几何驱动力，参与早期密度扰动的演化与宇宙大尺度结构的形成。结构生力不仅调控CMB的温度涨落模式，也影响星系、丝状结构和空洞在宇宙网中的分布。  

**English**:  
The Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) provides a panoramic view of temperature and density fluctuations in the early universe. Conventional explanations rely on inflationary theory, baryon acoustic oscillations, and dark matter distributions. However, subtle anisotropies and regularities in large-scale structure remain enigmatic. This paper proposes Structural Vital Force as a cross-scale geometric driving principle, influencing early density fluctuation evolution and the formation of cosmic large-scale structures. Structural Vital Force modulates temperature fluctuation patterns in the CMB and affects the distribution of galaxies, filaments, and voids in the cosmic web.
