# 结构生力与星系形成演化  
## Structural Vital Force and the Formation and Evolution of Galaxies

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
星系的形成与演化是宇宙学的重要研究课题，传统理论主要依赖引力坍缩、暗物质晕与气体动力学。然而，关于星系形态的多样性、螺旋结构的普遍性、以及星系在宇宙大尺度结构中的分布，现有模型仍存在解释不足。本文提出“结构生力”作为一种跨尺度几何驱动力，参与并调控星系从初始涨落到复杂形态的演化。结构生力不仅为星系螺旋臂提供内在对称约束，也可能解释星系并合后的自洽结构重建，以及宇宙网格状结构的自组织特征。  

**English**:  
The formation and evolution of galaxies are central topics in cosmology, traditionally explained through gravitational collapse, dark matter halos, and gas dynamics. However, existing models face limitations in explaining the diversity of galaxy morphologies, the ubiquity of spiral structures, and the distribution of galaxies within large-scale cosmic structures. This paper proposes "Structural Vital Force" as a cross-scale geometric driving principle that governs galaxy evolution from initial fluctuations to complex morphologies. Structural Vital Force not only provides intrinsic symmetry constraints for spiral arms but may also account for structural self-reconstruction following mergers and the emergent lattice-like features of the cosmic web.
