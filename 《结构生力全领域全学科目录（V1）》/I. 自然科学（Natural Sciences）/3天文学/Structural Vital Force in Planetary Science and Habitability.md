## Structural Vital Force in Planetary Science and Habitability

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
行星科学研究行星的形成、演化与环境条件，传统理论依赖引力聚合、热演化和地质过程来解释行星特性。然而，关于行星轨道稳定性、内部结构、表面与大气耦合机制，以及适宜生命存在的条件，仍存在未解之谜。本文提出“结构生力”作为一种跨尺度几何驱动力，可辅助解释行星的形成与内部结构稳定性，并为宜居条件提供新的空间约束视角。结构生力不仅影响行星自转、轨道共振及环系统的稳定，还可能调控液态水存在、气候稳定性及大气循环模式。  

**English**:  
Planetary science investigates the formation, evolution, and environmental conditions of planets, traditionally explained through gravitational aggregation, thermal evolution, and geological processes. However, unresolved questions remain regarding orbital stability, internal structures, surface–atmosphere coupling, and conditions suitable for life. This paper proposes Structural Vital Force as a cross-scale geometric driving principle that aids in explaining planetary formation, internal structural stability, and provides a novel spatial constraint perspective for habitability. Structural Vital Force influences planetary rotation, orbital resonances, and ring system stability, and may also regulate liquid water presence, climate stability, and atmospheric circulation patterns.
