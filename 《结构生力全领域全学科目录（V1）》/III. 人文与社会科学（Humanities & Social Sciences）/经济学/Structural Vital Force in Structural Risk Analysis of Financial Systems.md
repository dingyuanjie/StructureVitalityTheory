# 结构生力在金融系统结构风险分析中的应用  
## Structural Vital Force in Structural Risk Analysis of Financial Systems

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
金融系统的稳定性受到银行、投资机构、市场参与者和金融产品之间复杂网络耦合的影响。传统风险管理方法多基于局部指标或历史数据，难以捕捉系统性风险的跨网络传播机制。本文提出“结构生力”作为金融系统结构性风险分析的驱动力，通过节点耦合强度、螺旋化网络拓扑和交易反馈机制，识别潜在系统性风险源、评估风险传导路径并优化监管策略。生力网络提供结构化风险指标，为宏观金融监管与危机防控提供理论基础。  

**English**:  
The stability of financial systems is influenced by the complex network coupling among banks, investment institutions, market participants, and financial products. Traditional risk management methods often rely on local indicators or historical data, struggling to capture cross-network propagation mechanisms of systemic risk. This paper introduces Structural Vital Force as a driving principle for structural risk analysis in financial systems. Through node coupling strength, helical network topology, and transaction feedback mechanisms, it identifies potential systemic risk sources, assesses risk propagation paths, and optimizes regulatory strategies. The force network provides structured risk metrics, offering a theoretical foundation for macro-financial supervision and crisis prevention.

---

## 一、金融节点螺旋耦合 (Helical Coupling of Financial Nodes)

**中文**：  
金融网络中的节点耦合反映机构间的资本流动和风险传递：  
- 生力网络量化银行、投资机构和市场参与者之间的资金与交易耦合强度；  
- 螺旋化节点关系揭示跨层级风险传导与累积效应；  
- 多层耦合实现系统局部稳健与全局风险监测。  

**English**:  
Node coupling in financial networks reflects capital flows and risk transmission among institutions:  
- The force network quantifies the coupling strength of capital and transactions among banks, investment institutions, and market participants.  
- Helical node relationships reveal cross-level risk propagation and cumulative effects.  
- Multi-layer coupling enables local robustness and global risk monitoring.

---

## 二、螺旋拓扑与风险传导 (Helical Topology and Risk Propagation)

**中文**：  
金融系统的网络拓扑决定风险传导路径和系统脆弱性：  
- 生力网络评估节点中心性、耦合度和集群系数，识别关键风险节点；  
- 螺旋拓扑设计缓解系统性风险累积和链式传导；  
- 动态网络重构优化风险隔离和资本缓冲策略。  

**English**:  
The network topology of financial systems determines risk propagation paths and systemic vulnerability:  
- The force network evaluates node centrality, coupling degree, and clustering coefficients to identify critical risk nodes.  
- Helical topology design mitigates systemic risk accumulation and cascade propagation.  
- Dynamic network reconstruction optimizes risk isolation and capital buffer strategies.

---

## 三、系统稳定性与风险指标 (System Stability and Risk Metrics)

**中文**：  
系统稳定性评估依赖跨网络结构分析：  
- 生力网络提供结构性风险指标，如节点脆弱度、耦合敏感度和累积风险指数；  
- 模拟外部冲击下的风险扩散和市场反应；  
- 支持金融监管、压力测试和危机预警，实现系统稳健运行。  

**English**:  
System stability assessment relies on cross-network structural analysis:  
- The force network provides structural risk metrics such as node fragility, coupling sensitivity, and cumulative risk index.  
- Simulates risk diffusion and market responses under external shocks.  
- Supports financial supervision, stress testing, and crisis early warning, achieving robust system operation.

## 四、展望与应用 (Outlook and Applications)

**中文**：  
未来研究方向包括：  
1. 全球金融网络螺旋耦合分析，实现跨市场系统性风险建模；  
2. 自适应螺旋化监管策略，提高金融体系韧性与风险预警能力；  
3. 应用于宏观金融政策制定、跨机构风险管理和数字货币体系，实现高效、稳健和可调控的金融系统。  

**English**:  
Future research directions include:  
1. Helical coupling analysis of global financial networks for cross-market systemic risk modeling.  
2. Adaptive helical regulatory strategies to enhance financial system resilience and risk early warning capabilities.  
3. Applications in macro-financial policy making, inter-institutional risk management, and digital currency systems to achieve efficient, robust, and controllable financial operations.
