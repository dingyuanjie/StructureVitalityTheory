# 结构生力在科学哲学研究中的应用：统一理论的验证逻辑  
## Structural Vital Force in Philosophy of Science: The Verification Logic of a Unified Theory

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
科学哲学关注理论构建、验证逻辑和科学方法论。统一理论旨在整合自然现象、规律和学科体系，但传统验证方法多依赖经验归纳或特定实验，缺乏跨学科与全局一致性分析。本文引入“结构生力”理论作为统一理论构建与验证的逻辑框架，通过螺旋耦合、层级结构和反馈机制量化理论一致性、预测能力及实验可检验性，为科学方法提供新的哲学基础。  

**English**:  
Philosophy of science focuses on theory construction, verification logic, and scientific methodology. Unified theories aim to integrate natural phenomena, laws, and disciplinary frameworks, but traditional verification methods often rely on empirical induction or specific experiments, lacking cross-disciplinary and global consistency analysis. This paper introduces Structural Vital Force theory as a logical framework for constructing and verifying unified theories, quantifying theoretical consistency, predictive power, and experimental testability through helical coupling, hierarchical structures, and feedback mechanisms, providing a new philosophical foundation for scientific methodology.
