# 结构生力视角下的数论与斐波那契序列  
## Number Theory and Fibonacci Sequences from the Structural Vital Force Perspective

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
数论和斐波那契序列在自然界和工程结构中普遍存在，其与结构生力的关系为揭示系统自组织和稳定性提供新视角。本文基于“结构生力”理论，分析斐波那契序列、黄金角度及其在微观粒子、材料结构和宏观天体螺旋排列中的作用。通过数论方法与结构建模相结合，揭示生力分布的规律性，为科学研究和工程优化提供数学工具与理论支撑。  

**English**:  
Number theory and Fibonacci sequences are ubiquitous in nature and engineering structures, and their relationship with Structural Vital Force provides new insights into system self-organization and stability. Based on Structural Vital Force theory, this paper analyzes the role of Fibonacci sequences, golden angles, and their applications in microscopic particles, material structures, and macroscopic helical arrangements of celestial bodies. By combining number theory with structural modeling, it reveals regularities in force distribution, offering mathematical tools and theoretical support for scientific research and engineering optimization.
