# 结构生力视角下的分形几何与黄金角度  
## Fractal Geometry and Golden Angles from the Structural Vital Force Perspective

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
分形几何与黄金角度广泛存在于自然界、自组织系统及工程结构中，为揭示复杂结构的稳定性和优化提供新的理论工具。本文基于“结构生力”理论，分析分形自相似性、分形维数及黄金角对局部与全局受力分布的影响。通过数学建模和模拟实验，展示分形结构与螺旋生力耦合的规律性，并提出在工程设计、材料优化及生物系统建模中的应用前景。  

**English**:  
Fractal geometry and golden angles are widely present in nature, self-organizing systems, and engineering structures, providing novel theoretical tools to understand structural stability and optimization. Based on Structural Vital Force theory, this paper analyzes the effects of fractal self-similarity, fractal dimension, and golden angles on local and global force distribution. Through mathematical modeling and simulations, it demonstrates the regularity of fractal structures coupled with helical force and proposes applications in engineering design, material optimization, and biological system modeling.
