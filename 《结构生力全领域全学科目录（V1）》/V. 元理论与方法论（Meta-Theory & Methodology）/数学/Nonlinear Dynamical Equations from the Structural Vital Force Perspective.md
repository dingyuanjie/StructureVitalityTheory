# 结构生力视角下的非线性动力学方程  
## Nonlinear Dynamical Equations from the Structural Vital Force Perspective

---

## 摘要 (Abstract)

**中文**：  
非线性动力学方程广泛应用于物理、工程、经济和生物系统中，用于描述复杂系统的时空演化。本文基于“结构生力”理论，提出将结构生力引入非线性动力学模型的方法，分析其对系统稳定性、振荡模式及混沌行为的影响。通过数学推导与数值模拟，展示结构生力如何调控非线性系统的动力响应，并探讨其在工程优化、材料设计和复杂系统预测中的应用潜力。  

**English**:  
Nonlinear dynamical equations are widely used in physics, engineering, economics, and biological systems to describe the spatiotemporal evolution of complex systems. Based on Structural Vital Force theory, this paper introduces the incorporation of Structural Vital Force into nonlinear dynamical models, analyzing its impact on system stability, oscillatory patterns, and chaotic behavior. Through mathematical derivation and numerical simulation, it demonstrates how Structural Vital Force regulates the dynamical responses of nonlinear systems and discusses potential applications in engineering optimization, material design, and complex system forecasting.
